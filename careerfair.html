UMSI Career Fair

Preparing for the UMSI Career Fair

This year's Career Fair will kick off with an employer panel focused on the future of diversity, equity and inclusion in the workplace. A panel of thought leaders from diverse industries will share insights on overcoming challenges and creating effective strategies within the DEI landscape. The Career Development Office is also hosting a workshop to help you prepare for the Career Fair and get the most out of your experience.
